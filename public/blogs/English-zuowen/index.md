# A Meaningful Exchange

  It was such a pleasant surprise to receive a book from my American pen pal Tom, which happens to be his favorite. I felt I should send him something special in return.

  After careful consideration, I decided to send him a set of traditional Chinese paper-cuttings. I also wrote a note to express my gratitude for the book and my sincere wishes for him.

  This meaningful exchange represents our deep friendship and strengthens our bond. At the same time, this traditional Chinese artwork helps spread Chinese culture to the United States.
